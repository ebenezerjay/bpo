New Release

"Bears Everywhere"

Woodburned basswood and acrylic paint on canvas.

"Bears Everywhere" has just been released (on Thanksgiving Day) as an edition of 10. Each of the 10 prints are 20" by 24" on artist quality canvas. If you've spent time around Alaska's great bears this is a special artwork to own & treasure. Brian's works are only printed in Limited Edition runs - a great holiday gift and shipping is free to the lower 48. There are only 10 available and we do expect these to go fast - after all there is no better gift than Art!

"I was fortunate to be part of a Backcountry Journeys photo trip to Lake Clark National Park in 2018. I witnessed the same breathtaking beauty the photographers enjoyed. I felt compelled to create an Art piece bringing the highlights of my experience together. If you visit this place, you will gain a greater understanding of this piece. I hope you like it."

-Brian Odermatt

Next Release

"Alaskan Playground"

"Alaskan Playground" will be released on December 10th, 2020. This is a must have collector's item for anyone who has spent time in and around Homer, Alaska. Make sure to get on Brian's email list and be the first to get a look at this piece when it is released!

"This canvas painting is based on a camping and kayaking trip to Grewingk glacier. It's near Homer Alaska, across Kachemak bay. After jumping off the water taxi and unloading our kayaks and gear, we spotted a mother black bear with her cubs across a small field from our camp. The next morning in a mixture of thick fog and bright sunlight we started our three mile hike to the glacier. I was stunned by the rich thick forest full of mushrooms, flowers, and mosses. Roughly halfway into our hike we encountered a mother black bear with her playful cubs on the trail. We waited patiently as they slowly made their way into the thick forest. After arriving at the lake in some warm sunshine, my friend and I put on our dry-suits and went for a swim. It was a pleasure and unique experience to enjoy ice water and floating icebergs. We sat peacefully on the shore for hours taking in the extraordinary beauty. I knew then, I was going to create an Art piece of my experience. My painting features the vigilant mother black bear taking her playful cubs to the lake for a swim. The painting is nearing completion and will be released shortly. This will also be offered in a very limited number of prints. Thanks for your interest."

Artwork Release Date: 12/10/2020
